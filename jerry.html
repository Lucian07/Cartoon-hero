This is Rick:

Rick Sanchez is the titular anti-heroic main protagonist of Rick and Morty. He is a genius scientist whose alcoholism and reckless, nihilistic behavior are a source of concern for his family.
He is from Earth dimension C-137 and is the father of Beth Smith, the grandfather of Morty Smith and Summer Smith.
